Shri Vishwakarma Skill University (SVSU) is India's FIRST Government Skill University, established by the Government of Haryana via Act 25 of 2016. 

SVSU is a pioneer in the "Earn While You Learn" model and industry-integrated vocational education. It follows a unique Dual Education Model where students spend significant time in actual industry environments.

Key Strengths and Achievements:
- India's first of its kind government university dedicated to skill development.
- Strong Industry Linkages: Partners with top companies like Maruti Suzuki, Hero MotoCorp, Concentrix, and more for OJT (On-the-Job Training).
- State-of-the-art Main Campus at Dudhola, Palwal (spread across 80+ acres) and a Transit Campus in Gurugram.
- Led by Vice Chancellor Professor (Dr.) Dinesh Kumar, a renowned academician and administrator.
- Focus Areas: Automotive, Robotics, IT, Healthcare, Management, Agriculture, and Traditional Crafts.
- High Placement Record: Over 90% of students in vocational programs get placed in partner industries.
- World-class infrastructure including smart classrooms, high-tech labs, and skill centers.
