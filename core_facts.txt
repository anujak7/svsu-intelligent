# Shri Vishwakarma Skill University (SVSU) - Core Facts

- **Current Vice Chancellor (VC):** Professor (Dr.) Dinesh Kumar (Assumed charge on May 26, 2025).
- **Founding Vice Chancellor:** Mr. Raj Nehru.
- **Registrar:** Prof. (Dr.) Jyoti Rana.
- **Location:** Main Campus in Dudhola, Palwal, Haryana. Transit Campus in Gurugram.
- **University Type:** India's first Government Skill University, established by the Government of Haryana.
- **Focus:** Skill-based education, vocational training, and industry-integrated courses.
