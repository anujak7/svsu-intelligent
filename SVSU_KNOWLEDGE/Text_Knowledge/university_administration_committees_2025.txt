## PART 1: LEADERSHIP MESSAGES & PHILOSOPHY

### Vice Chancellor's Vision (Shri Raj Nehru - Founding VC)
- SVSU is India’s very first Skill University.
- Philosophy: Skilling was the backbone of ancient civilizations (Takshila/Nalanda). SVSU aims to reclaim that global recognition.
- Chariot Symbolism: The university architecture (6 academic blocks as horses, admin block as wheels) represents the Chariot of Lord Krishna, symbolizing purposeful action (Karma).

### Registrar's Message (Prof. Jyoti Rana)
- Focus: Change is constant; skills are the edge.
- SVSU Strengths: Training, pedagogy, On-Job Training (OJT), industry integration, vocational framework, and affordable high-quality education.
- Team Commitment: Dedicated to the vision of a "distinct" skill university.
- Email: registrar@svsu.ac.in

### Dean Academic Affairs Message (Prof. (Dr.) Vikram Singh)
- Mission: Integrating technology, capacity building, and truthful values to serve humanity.
- Focus: Experience-based education, moral depth, and industry-led research opportunities.
- Email: dean.academics@svsu.ac.in

## PART 2: UNIVERSITY GOVERNANCE & COUNCILS

### 1. University Court
- **Chairperson**: The Chancellor (Ex-Officio).
- **Secretary**: The Registrar (Ex-Officio).
- **Functions**: Review broad policies, approve annual reports, budget, and accounts; advise the Chancellor.
- **Quorum**: 3/5th of members.
- **Meeting History**: Meetings 1 to 8 (2019 - 2026).

### 2. Executive Council (EC)
- **Chairperson**: The Vice Chancellor (Ex-Officio).
- **Secretary**: The Registrar (Ex-Officio).
- **Powers**: Control revenue/property, create/abolish posts, appoint professors/staff, amend statutes, maintain discipline, approve finances.
- **Financial Note**: Decisions involving financial liability require the consent of the Administrative Secretary to Govt, Haryana, Finance Department.
- **Meeting History**: Meetings 1 to 43 (2017 - 2026).

### 3. Planning Board
- **Chairperson**: The Vice Chancellor (Ex-Officio).
- **Functions**: Advise on planning, development, and standards of education and research.
- **Members**: High-standing experts from industry and academia (AICTE, IIT, IIM, MSDI nominees).

### 4. Finance Committee
- **Chairperson**: The Vice Chancellor (Ex-Officio).
- **Functions**: Scrutinize expenditure proposals, submit annual budgets, recommend creation of posts.
- **Note**: No expenditure can be incurred without Finance Committee approval.

## PART 7: SPECIALIZED ADMISSION CELLS

### 1. DAKSH (Competitive Exam Coaching)
- **What it is**: An Educational Start-Up under SVSU Skill Innovators Foundation.
- **Focus**: Hybrid coaching for UPSC, HSC, SSC, Banking, and Railways.
- **Eligibility**: Final year graduates or degree holders.
- **Scholarship**: Up to 90% waiver in fees based on "Daksh Scholarship Cum Admission Test".
- **Annual Fee**: Rs. 45,000 (payable in 3 installments).
- **Key Features**: Guidance by serving IAS/IPS/PCS officers, personality development, and e-learning platform.

### 2. Cell for International Students
- **Quota**: 5% supernumerary seats in UG/PG programs.
- **Requirement**: AIU Equivalence Certificate is mandatory.
- **Visa**: Only Student Visa/Research Visa is accepted (No Tourist/Medical Visa).
- **Portal**: Must register on https://studyinindia.gov.in/
- **Reporting**: Foreign students must report to FRRO within 14 days of arrival.
