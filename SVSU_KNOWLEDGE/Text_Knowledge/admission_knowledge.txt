=== ADMISSIONS: INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS ===

GUIDELINES & MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS:
- Equivalence Certificate: Mandatory degree equivalence issued by Association of Indian Universities (AIU).
- Study in India Portal: Mandatory registration on https://studyinindia.gov.in/ after receiving provisional admission letter.
- Visa Type: Valid Student Visa/Research Visa in the name of Shri Vishwakarma Skill University is mandatory. Tourist/X/Medical visas are not accepted.
- Translation: All documents must be translated into English.
- Attestation: Marksheets must be attested by the Ministry of Education or Indian Embassy of the concerned country.
- Reporting: Students must report to the Foreigner Regional Registration Office (FRRO) within 14 days of arrival.
- Work Restriction: Students are not allowed to work with any Institute/Firm during their study period.
- No Refund: Fees once paid are non-refundable.

RESERVATION OF SEATS:
- 5% supernumerary seats are reserved for Foreign Nationals in each UG/PG program (except AICTE programs).

HOW TO APPLY:
1. Via Study in India Portal (https://studyinindia.gov.in)
2. Directly via University Website (https://www.svsu.ac.in)

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR APPLICATION:
- Proof of Date of Birth (Passport/10th/Birth Cert).
- Marks Sheets/Transcripts of qualifying exams.
- Passport (valid for at least 6 months).
- Address Proof, Photo, and Digital Signature.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED AT ADMISSION:
- Signed hard copy of online application.
- Ten passport-size photographs.
- Original transcripts attested by Ministry of Education/Indian Embassy.
- Conduct & Character Certificate.
- Valid Student/Research Visa.
- Fitness Certificate (HIV/COVID-free) by a registered practitioner.
- AIU Equivalence Certificate.

ADMISSION PROCESS:
- Screening by Departmental Admission Committee based on eligibility, merit, and seat availability.
- Provisional Admission Offer Letter sent via email.
- Extension for reporting can be requested via email for valid reasons.
