[SVSU GUEST HOUSE RULES & COE EXTENDED - 2025-26]

### University Guest House & Transit Accommodation Rules
- **Purpose:** Residential facility for visitors and academic purposes at University Campus. Strictly not for private use.
- **Free Stay (Exemptions):** 
  - Vice-Chancellor (VC) can exempt any person.
  - Members of Court, Executive Council, Academic Council, Finance Committee, Selection Committee, NAAC Officials (on official visit).
  - Serving VCs and Registrars of State/Central Universities (official visit).
  - SVSU staff for university events (must be approved within 3 days).
  - *Note: Exempted guests pay a token amount of Rs. 10 per night.*
- **Room Tariffs (Per Night):**
  - SVSU Staff & family members: Rs. 500/-
  - Haryana/Central Govt. Employees (official programs): Rs. 600/-
  - Guests for official events/seminars/Research Scholars: Rs. 700/-
  - Others (not covered above): Rs. 1000/-
  - Day use room (Max 6 hours): Rs. 300/- (Full tariff after 6 hours).
  - VIP Suite: Rs. 2500/- (Requires VC permission).
  - Dining Hall Rental (Private functions for SVSU staff): Rs. 2500/- + GST.
- **Booking Rules:**
  - Max 10 days stay in a month.
  - Check-in: 12:00 Noon | Check-out: 11:00 AM.
  - 100% advance payment via electronic mode (No cash accepted).
  - ID Proof mandatory at check-in. Booking form should be submitted 5 days in advance.
- **Cancellation & Refund:**
  - 100% refund if cancelled 24 hours before check-in.
  - No refund on the date of booking or 'No Show'.

### Centre of Excellence (COE) - Extended Details
- **Vice-Chancellor:** Prof. Dinesh Kumar.
- **Mission:** India's first Skill University bridging academic learning and industry requirements via IIDEM model.
- **Daikin Centre of Excellence:** 
  - Focus: Air Conditioning and Refrigeration (HVAC).
  - Facilities: VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume), RA (Residential), SA (SkyAir), AHU (Air Handling Unit), Commercial Cold Storage.
  - Courses: Job-oriented certification for Maintenance Technicians.
- **Short Term Courses (General COE):**
  - **Durations:** 3 Days | 1-2 Weeks | 1-3 Months | 3-6 Months.
  - **Areas:** CNC Machining, Advance Welding (TIG/MIG/ARC), Mechatronics Maintenance, PLC Programming, Solar PV Systems, EV Drive Systems.
  - **Focus:** Upskilling and Reskilling for industry professionals and students.
