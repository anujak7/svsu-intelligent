[SVSU ADMISSION RULES & PROCEDURES - 2025-26]

### Admission Procedure
1. For Out-Campus Programs: The student must have attained the age of 16 years/18 years at the time of commencement of OJT subject to specific programs offered under different Skill Faculties. In case of deviation, the decision of Competent Authority shall be final.
2. For In-Campus Programs: there is no minimum age of applying for admissions.
3. No. of seats may increase or decrease without any personal communication to the candidates.
4. In such Programs where the industry partner has yet to be finalized, detailed information will be released later.
5. The stipend will be paid at the discretion of the industry partners and will be paid exclusively during the OJT period. It is not a matter of right.
6. If the number of students admitted in a Program is less than 25% of sanctioned intake, Program may not be offered. However, fees will be refunded on the request of the student as per guidelines of UGC/AICTE or the student may be allowed to select other Programs based on his or her eligibility subject to availability of seats, however student will have to comply with the selection procedure.
7. The Programs are offered in collaboration and consultation with Industry partners. If proposed industry partner does not accord consent, the Program may not be offered session 2025- 26. The industry partners are tentative and may change.
8. Relaxation in minimum eligibility marks for reserved category (SC/ST) Candidates: A General candidate requires 50%, a 5% relaxation means the requirement is 47.50% (calculated as 50 - 2.50), not 45%.

### Distribution and Reservation of Seats
- All India Open Category (AIC): 15% of sanctioned intake.
- EWS (Central Govt): 10% of AIC seats.
- State Quota (Haryana): 85% of sanctioned intake.
  - Haryana Open General Category (HOGC): 50% of State Quota (42.5% of total).
  - EWS (Haryana): 10% of HOGC (4.25% of total).
  - Reserved Categories of Haryana: 50% of State Quota (42.5% of total).
    - Scheduled Caste (SC): 20% of State Quota (17% total). Split 50:50 between SC and Deprived SC.
    - Backward Classes (BC-A): 16% of State Quota (13.6% total).
    - Backward Classes (BC-B): 11% of State Quota (9.35% total).
    - Physically Handicapped (PH): 3% of State Quota (2.55% total).

### Supernumerary Seats
- Dudhola Village (Land Givers): 02 seats in programs with intake up to 30.
- J&K Migrants: 01 seat in each program.
- Women Candidates: 10% seats in each program (Except B.Tech/M.Tech/MBA/Lateral Entry).
- Industry Sponsored Women: 01 seat in each program (Except B.Tech/M.Tech/MBA/Lateral Entry).
- PM Cares Students: As per govt guidelines.

### Admission Guidelines & Counseling
- All admissions in Physical mode only. No online counseling.
- Fees must be deposited via online mode (Debit/Credit/Net banking/UPI).
- If a candidate misses fee deposit, they can only participate in "On the Spot" counseling later.
- Vacant BC(B) seats filled from BC(A) and vice-versa after 2nd counseling.
- Vacant SC seats filled from SC(D) and vice-versa.
- Reserved seats (other than SC) vacant after 3rd merit list are opened to Haryana General Category.

### Selection Criteria
1. MBA and BBA: As per AICTE guidelines for session.
2. M.Tech, B.Tech & Diploma Engineering: As per HSTES (Department of Technical Education, Haryana) guidelines.
3. Industry OJT Programs (Non-AICTE): 80% weightage to qualifying exam marks + 20% weightage to Personal Interview/Group Discussion.
4. Other Programs: Based on marks in qualifying exam.

### Instructions for Interview/Test
- Candidates 7 times the sanctioned intake are called for interviews based on merit.
- Must bring original documents + 2 sets of self-attested photocopies.
- Rejection in Interview/GD means disqualification from that selection stage regardless of academic merit.

### Online Application Form Fee
- AIC/HOGC: Rs. 500/-
- BC/EWS of Haryana: Rs. 250/-
- J&K Migrants: Rs. 250/-
- Female/SC/ST/PwD of Haryana: NIL (FREE)
- Dudhola Candidates: Rs. 100/-
- Saksham Yuva: Rs. 50/-
- AVBKY (Wards of Martyrs): Rs. 50/-
- Transgender: NIL (FREE)

### Registration & Fine Rules
- Late fee fine: Rs. 10 per day.
- Re-admission fee: Rs. 1000/- + fine.
- Attendance requirement: 75% for Theory, 90% for OJT.

### General Instructions
- AADHAR linking with bank account is mandatory.
- Legal disputes jurisdiction: Palwal/Gurugram.
- Entire Campus is a 'No Smoking Zone'.
- Uniform is mandatory for all students.
- Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP) copy is mandatory for Haryana candidates.
