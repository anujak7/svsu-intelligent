[SVSU VICE CHANCELLOR'S MESSAGE & VISION]

### Vice Chancellor's Message
- **Vice Chancellor:** Prof. Dinesh Kumar.
- **Vision:** India’s first Government Skill University dedicated to high-quality skill-based education.
- **Motto:** “योगः कर्मसु कौशलम्” (Yoga is Excellence at Work).
- **Alignment:** Aligned with national programs like Skill India, Digital India, Make in India, Semicon India, and PLI schemes.
- **Educational Model:** 
  - Aligned with National Education Policy (NEP) 2020.
  - **KG to PG Model:** Ensures a seamless continuum of learning from foundational to higher education (SVSU Senior Secondary School to PhD).
  - **Focus Areas:** Robotics & Automation, IoT, Semiconductor Technology, Electric Vehicles, Sustainable Engineering, AI, Data Analytics, Cloud Computing, Precision Farming.
  - **Industry Integration:** Industry Integrated Dual Education Model (IIDEM) with experiential learning and On-the-Job Training (OJT).

### University Pillars
- **Bridging Employability:** Mission to produce professionals, visionaries, and entrepreneurs.
- **Inclusivity:** Multidisciplinary, flexible education with multiple entry-exit options.
- **Self-Reliance:** Preparing students as ethical leaders and innovators for a self-reliant India.
