
# SHRI VISHWAKARMA SKILL UNIVERSITY (SVSU)
# POLICY FOR PROMOTION OF RESEARCH & RECRUITMENT RULES (2024-2025)

## PART 1: POLICY FOR PROMOTION OF RESEARCH
(Approved in 31st Executive Council Meeting - 18.01.2024)

### Introduction
SVSU aims to promote a research ecosystem in manufacturing, IT, textile, design, logistics, automation, agriculture, and maintenance. The policy applies to all faculty, scholars, and students.

### Key Objectives
1. Strengthen institutional capacity for industry connect and collaborations.
2. Organize research events (summits, conferences, workshops).
3. Identify and solve industry problems (productivity, design, quality).
4. Motivate for patents and national/international paper presentations.
5. Secure extramural grants and provide Seed Money for internal research.
6. Recognize best performers in research and consultancy.
7. Promote IPR (Intellectual Property Rights) culture.

### Research Administration (RDC)
- Research & Development Cell (RDC) functions under the Vice-Chancellor.
- Advisory Council and five committees mandated by UGC guidelines.
- Respective Deans/Chairpersons coordinate research in Faculties/Departments.

### Research Funding & Resources
#### 1. Innovation Fund
- Rs. 5,00,000 for each skill faculty (block of 2 years).
- Purpose: Prototype development, industry-based problems, social immersion projects, and IPR filing.

#### 2. Seed Money (UFRG - University Funded Research Program)
- Total Corpus: Rs. 50 Lakhs.
- Grant Amount: Maximum Rs. 3 Lakhs per project (block of 3 years).
- Eligibility: Regular faculty/instructors with at least 3 years of service remaining.
- Outcome: At least one research paper in indexed journals (SCI/Scopus/UGC Care).

#### 3. Extramural Funds
- Faculty expected to submit projects to external funding agencies (Govt/NGO/Industry).
- R&D cell monitors progress and helps with Utilization Certificates (UC).

### Research Awards & Incentives
- **Five Best Researcher Awards** (Rs. 5100 each) per faculty annually.
- Categories:
  1. Best Researcher: Publications (SCI/Scopus/UGC Care).
  2. Best Researcher: Impact Factor (Clarivate Analytics).
  3. Best Researcher: h-index/i-index (Google Scholar profiles must be public).
  4. Best Researcher: Projects (Sanctioned value > Rs. 3 Lakhs).
  5. Best Researcher: Collaboration (Joint publications/projects).
- **Lifetime Achievement Award**: For teachers retiring within a specific timeframe with significant contributions to SVSU.
- **Consultancy Reward**: Rs. 5100 for each consultancy work done.
- **Qualification Development**: Reward of max Rs. 20,000 for developing futuristic job role qualifications.

## PART 2: ACADEMIC INTEGRITY & PLAGIARISM (UGC 2018)
SVSU follows the "UGC (Promotion of Academic Integrity and Prevention of Plagiarism in HEIs) Regulations, 2018".

### Similarity Checks & Levels
- **Exclusions**: Quoted work, references, bibliography, generic terms, laws, and standard equations.
- **Levels of Plagiarism**:
  - Level 0: Up to 10% (Minor similarities, no penalty).
  - Level 1: >10% to 40% (Student: Revise script within 6 months; Faculty: Withdraw manuscript).
  - Level 2: >40% to 60% (Student: Debarred for 1 year; Faculty: Withdraw manuscript, deny 1 increment, no supervision for 2 years).
  - Level 3: >60% (Student: Registration cancelled; Faculty: Withdraw manuscript, deny 2 increments, no supervision for 3 years).

### Integrity Panels
1. **Departmental Academic Integrity Panel (DAIP)**: Chaired by Head of Dept; investigates initial allegations.
2. **Institutional Academic Integrity Panel (IAIP)**: Chaired by Pro-VC/Dean; reviews DAIP reports and decides penalties.

## PART 3: RECRUITMENT RULES (NON-TEACHING/TECHNICAL)
(Approved in 28th Executive Council - 26.09.2023)

### Appointing Authorities
- **Group A & B**: Executive Council (on recommendation of Establishment Committee).
- **Group C & D**: Vice-Chancellor (on recommendation of Departmental Recruitment/Promotion Committee).

### Method of Recruitment
- Direct Recruitment (Open competition, advertised in 2 national dailies).
- Promotion (Seniority-cum-Merit; at least 70% "Good" or above ACRs).
- Deputation (From Central/State Govt or Universities).

### Specific Post Eligibility (Selected Examples)
- **Controller of Examination (FPL-14)**: Master's (55%), 15 years exp (Academic Level 12+) or 15 years Admin exp (8 years as Deputy Registrar). Max age: 55.
- **Deputy Registrar (FPL-12)**: Master's (55%), 5 years exp as Assistant Registrar (FPL-10). Max age: 50.
- **Executive Engineer Civil (FPL-11)**: First Class B.E/B.Tech, 8 years exp as SDO. Max age: 50.
- **Assistant Registrar (FPL-10)**: Master's (55%), 5 years exp as Superintendent (FPL-7). Max age: 45.
- **Resident Medical Officer (FPL-10)**: MBBS degree, registered with MCI. Max age: 45.
- **Superintendent (FPL-7)**: Bachelor's (50%), 5 years exp as Deputy Superintendent/Assistant. Max age: 45.
- **Assistant (FPL-6)**: Bachelor's (50%), 5 years exp as Clerk/DEO. Max age: 42.
- **JE Civil/Electrical (FPL-6)**: B.E/B.Tech (60%) with 2 years exp, or Diploma (60%) with 5 years exp. Max age: 42.
- **Clerk/Clerk-cum-DEO (FPL-2)**: Graduate, must qualify computer/typing test (30 wpm English). Max age: 42.

### General Terms
- **Reservation**: Strictly follows Haryana State Govt Policy.
- **Probation**: 01 Year for most regular appointments.
- **Age Relaxation**: As per State Govt norms for reserved categories.
- **Joining Time**: Normally 1-2 months; can be extended on merit.
