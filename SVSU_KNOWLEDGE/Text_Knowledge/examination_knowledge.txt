=== SVSU COMPREHENSIVE EXAMINATION ORDINANCE (FULL SUMMARY) ===

1. ACADEMIC CALENDAR & ENROLMENT:
- Examinations are conducted according to the Academic Calendar prepared by the Dean and COE.
- Every student is allotted a unique Enrolment Number upon admission.
- Examination forms and fees must be submitted within specified schedules (December/January for Odd; May/June for Even semesters).

2. ADMIT CARDS & ENTRY:
- Students must produce a valid Admit Card to enter the examination hall.
- Duplicate Admit Cards: Available for Rs. 250 (lost) or Rs. 500 (with FIR for theft).
- Permission to appear can be withdrawn if the student is found ineligible.

3. STAFFING & CENTRES:
- Examination Centres are designated by the COE.
- Superintendent-in-Chief: Usually the Head of the Institution.
- Flying Squad / Observes: Appointed to ensure fairness and prevent malpractice.

4. GRADING SYSTEM & PASSING MARKS:
- Minimum passing marks: 40% (aggregate of Theory and Internal Assessment).
- Grading Table:
  * 90-100: O (Outstanding) - 10 GP
  * 80-89: A+ (Excellent) - 9 GP
  * 70-79: A (Very Good) - 8 GP
  * 60-69: B+ (Good) - 7 GP
  * 55-59: B (Above Average) - 6 GP
  * 46-54: C (Average) - 5 GP
  * 40-45: P (Pass) - 4 GP
  * <40: F (Fail) - 0 GP

5. RE-EVALUATION RULES:
- Theory papers can be re-evaluated within 15 days of result declaration.
- If re-evaluation increases marks by more than 15%, a second evaluator is consulted.
- Re-evaluation fee: As prescribed intermittently in the university fee schedule.

6. GOLD MEDAL & AWARDS:
- Minimum requirement: CGPA of 6.32 (1st Division).
- Eligibility: Student must pass all subjects in the regular period and the very first attempt (no re-appears).

7. UNFAIR MEANS (UMC) PENALTIES:
- Cancellation of the specific paper OR the entire semester examination.
- Debarring from university examinations for 1 to 3 years depending on severity.
- Use of mobile phones or smart devices in the hall is strictly prohibited.

8. STUDENT GRIEVANCES & IMPROVEMENT:
- Students can appeal against question paper discrepancies within 7 days.
- Improvement Exams: Allowed for specific cases to upgrade grades/CGPA.
- Photocopies of Answer Sheets: Can be requested within 30 days of result.
