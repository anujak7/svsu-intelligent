[SVSU STUDENT WELFARE, SCHOLARSHIPS & COMMITTEES - 2025-26]

### Department of Student's Welfare (DSW)
- **Dean:** Prof. (Dr.) Kulwant Singh.
- **Role:** Bridge between students and university vision. Focuses on academic support, mental health counseling, mentorship, and extracurricular activities.

### Cells, Clubs and Committees
1. **Foreign Students Cell:** Facilitates international students during admission and studies.
2. **Centre for Competitive Examinations:** Coaching and guidance for competitive exams.
3. **Career Counselling and Placement Cell:** Skill development and placement facilitation.
4. **Youth Welfare Committee:** Youth coordination, creativity, and tracking student attendance.
5. **Minority Cell:** Assistance to Minority, SC, and ST students (counseling, skills).
6. **Youth Red Cross Committee:** YRC activities.
7. **SC/ST/OBC Cell:** Addressing grievances of reserved category students.
8. **Cell for Differently Abled:** Support for students with disabilities.
9. **NSS & NCC Cells:** National Service Scheme and National Cadet Corps activities.
10. **Technical Club:** Hackathons, Robo wars, Coding, Automation workshops.
11. **Sports Club:** Indoor/Outdoor games, Yoga, Zumba, University trips.
12. **Literary Club:** Debates, GDs, Poetry, Quizzes.
13. **Cultural Club:** Dance, Music, Fashion shows, Festivals.
14. **Student Voters Club:** Promoting voting awareness.
15. **Photography Club (Dadasaheb Phalke):** Photography, Theatre, Painting, Skit.

### Schemes and Scholarships
- **World Skill Competition Fee Waiver:**
  - International Medal: 100% Tuition Fee Waiver.
  - National Medal: 50% Tuition Fee Waiver.
  - State Medal: 25% Tuition Fee Waiver.
- **Har-Chhatravratti Portal:** Centralized Haryana portal for 16 scholarship schemes (Post Matric SC/BC, Merit scholarships, etc.). Requires Parivar Pehchan Patra (PPP).
- **APJ Abdul Kalam Scholarship:**
  - Sponsored by 'The Oswals' (Roop Automotives): Full semester fee support for brilliant minds.
  - Sponsored by 'Concentrix': Full year academic fee support for 50 students.
- **Knorr & Bremse Scholarship:** For financially weaker and meritorious students.
- **National Scholarship Portal (NSP):** Centralized portal for various Govt. schemes.

### Anti-Ragging Guidelines
- **Penalty:** Fine up to Rs. 50,000/- and up to 3 years rigorous imprisonment.
- **Deterrent Measures:** Cancellation of admission, suspension, withholding results, debarring from placements, and lodging FIR.
- **Anti-Ragging Squads:** Led by Deans of respective faculties (SFET, SFMSR, SFASH, SFA).
