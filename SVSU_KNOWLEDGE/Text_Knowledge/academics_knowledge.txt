- Director: Prof. S.K. Sinha (iqaccell@svsu.ac.in)
- Deputy Director: Dr. Samarth Singh (samarth.singh@svsu.ac.in)
- Role: Catalyst for continuous improvement in academic, administrative, and co-curricular processes.
- Functions: Quality benchmarks, Academic & Administrative Audits, AQAR preparation.

2. “SUPER 30” - ENTREPRENEURSHIP DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM (EDP):
- Collaboration: SVSU & SIDBI (Small Industries Development Bank of India).
- Structure: 6 months course + 1 year incubation at SVSU campous.
- Capacity: 30 participants with innovative business ideas.
- Phases: Ideation/Validation -> Business Planning -> Execution/Growth -> Pitch/Launch.
- Focus: Business and Personality development, Communication, Industry engagement, Mentorship.
- Contact: edprogram@svsu.ac.in

3. DAKSH (Developing Administrative Knowledge for Society and Humanity):
- Goal: Competitive exam preparation (UPSC, HSC, SSC, Banking, Railways) for youth (esp. rural & lower financial status).
- Features: Officer guidance (IAS, IPS, Forest Service), virtual & physical classes, career counseling, e-learning platform.
- Scholarship: Up to 90% scholarship based on entrance test marks.
- Total Seats: 140.
- Annual Program Fee: Rs 45,000/- (plus GST).
