=== SVSU FACILITIES: HOSTEL & LIBRARY ===

1. HOSTEL FACILITIES (GIRLS & BOYS):
- Accommodation: Six-story buildings for both Boys and Girls.
- Room Types: 4-seater, 3-seater, and single-seater rooms (allotted based on academic program level).
- Amenities: Indoor/outdoor games, common rooms with LED TVs, study/reading rooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, RO water coolers.
- Security: 24/7 security guards and CCTV surveillance.

HOSTEL ADMISSION RULES & ELIGIBILITY:
- Eligibility: No accommodation for students residing within 30 KMs (for Boys) and 15 KMs (for Girls) from the University Hostel Campus.
- Application: Online via university website (www.svsu.ac.in). Requires signed declaration by student and parents, and recommendation by the concerned Dean.

HOSTEL FEE & SECURITY (Session 2024-25):
- Hostel Security: Rs 5,000/- (Refundable)
- Hostel Mess Security: Rs 5,000/- (Refundable)
- Hostel Charges: Rs 11,000/- (Per semester)
- Total (at admission): Rs 21,000/- (Note: Mess charges are extra).
