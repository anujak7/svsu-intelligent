=== SVSU NON-TEACHING RECRUITMENT RULES & POSTS ===

1. APPOINTING AUTHORITIES:
- Executive Council: For Group A & Group B posts (on recommendation of Establishment Committee).
- Vice-Chancellor: For Group C & Group D posts (on recommendation of Departmental Recruitment/Promotion Committee).

2. METHODS OF RECRUITMENT:
- Direct Recruitment (Open competition via advertisement).
- Promotion (Seniority-cum-merit basis).
- Deputation (From Central/State Govt. or Universities).

3. KEY NON-TEACHING POSTS & SCALES:
- Group A: Controller of Examination (FPL-14), Deputy Registrar (FPL-12), Sr. Accounts Officer (FPL-11), Assistant Registrar (FPL-10), Resident Medical Officer (FPL-10).
- Group B: SDO Electrical/Civil (FPL-9), Network Administrator (FPL-9), Website Administrator (FPL-9), Superintendent (FPL-7), Section Officer (FPL-7).
- Group C: Personal Secretary, Accountant, Translator, JE (Civil/Electrical/Horticulture), Law Assistant, Staff Nurse (FPL-6), Pharmacist (FPL-6), Clerk/DEO (FPL-2), Store Keeper (FPL-2).
- Group D: Hostel Attendant (FPL-1), Peon, Mali, Cook, Sweeper, Laboratory Attendant/MTS (FPL-DL).

4. GENERAL ELIGIBILITY:
- Age Limits: Generally below 50 years for Group A/B; below 42 years for Group C/D (Relaxations as per Haryana Govt rules).
- Educational: Ranges from Master's/Ph.D for senior roles to Matric/Class IV for operational roles. Knowledge of Hindi/Sanskrit up to Matric is generally mandatory.
