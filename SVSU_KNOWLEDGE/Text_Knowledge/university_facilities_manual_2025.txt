[SVSU UNIVERSITY FACILITIES & GUIDELINES - 2025-26]

### Hostel Facilities
- **Overview:** 6-story buildings for both Girls and Boys hostels. Includes indoor/outdoor games, common room, study room, reading room (newspapers/magazines), LED TVs, high-speed Wi-Fi, Water coolers (RO), CCTV, and security guards.
- **Accommodation:** 4-seaters, 3-seaters, and single-seaters available.
- **Distance Eligibility Rule:** No Hostel Accommodation will be given to any Student whose permanent residence is situated within 30 KMs (for Boys) and 15 KMs (for Girls) from the University Hostel Campus.
- **Capacity:**
  - Boys Hostel (Kailash Bhawan): 490 total (90 single, 80 three-seater, 40 four-seater).
  - Girls Hostel (Himadri Bhawan): 490 total (90 single, 80 three-seater, 40 four-seater).
- **Fees & Security (Session 2024-25):**
  - Hostel Security: Rs. 5000/- (Refundable)
  - Hostel Mess Security: Rs. 5000/- (Refundable)
  - Hostel Charges: Rs. 11000/- per semester.
  - **Total One-time Admission Amount:** Rs. 21000/- (Note: This does NOT include daily mess charges).- **Step-by-Step Procedure to Apply for Online Hostel Admission:**
  1. Click on **Apply online** via University Website (www.svsu.ac.in).
  2. Fill all details correctly. Note: No edit option is available after submission.
  3. Check all required boxes and click "I Agree" on statements.
  4. Submit the form.
  5. You will receive a text message with **User ID and Password** for re-login.
  6. Click "Pay Now" and then "Proceed".
  7. Pay the fee via Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI, or Bharat QR.
  8. A confirmation email with a fee receipt will be sent.
  9. Click "Download Receipt".
  10. Download the Application PDF and Fee Receipt.
  11. Take prints, sign declarations (by student and parents), and get them recommended by the **Dean** of your respective faculty.
  12. Attach required documents, Fee Receipt, and **Anti-Ragging Affidavit**.
  13. Deposit the complete form physically at the Hostel office as per instructions.

### Centre of Excellence (COE)
- **Mission:** Established in 2023 to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical application.
- **Laboratories at COE:**
  1. **Advance CNC Machining:** Realistic virtual machining via CNC Simulator, FANUC and SIEMENS controllers.
     - Faculty: Dr. Sanjay Kumar (MSI), Mr. Durgesh Gupta (SI), Mr. Gurdeep Kumar (SI).
  2. **Advance Welding:** Equipped with ESAB (American-Swedish) technologies (TIG, MIG, SMAW, Oxy-Acetylene).
     - Faculty: Ms. Ekta (Asst. Prof), Mr. Brijesh Poswal (Foreman), Mohd. Saalim (SI).
  3. **Advance Mechatronics:** FESTO and SMC technologies. Pneumatic/Hydraulic setups, PLC training, Industry 4.0 modules.
     - Faculty: Mr. Dinesh (Asst. Prof), Mr. Jagbir Singh (MSI).
  4. **Advance Electrical Lab:** Power converters, PWM techniques, EV technology, Green Technology.
     - Faculty: Dr. Preeti (Asst. Prof), Mr. Puneet Raghav (Foreman).
  5. **Advance Solar Lab:** Photovoltaic and solar thermal technologies, energy storage.
     - Faculty: Dr. Sunil Kumar Sharma (Asst. Prof), Mr. Parveen (SI).

### Attendance & Leave Rules
- **Minimum Attendance:** 75% for Theory/Practical, 90% for On-the-Job Training (OJT).
- **Absence Rule:** Name struck off if absent for 15 days (Theory) or 7 days (OJT) continuously.
- **Re-admission:** Rs. 500/- fee (if approved).
- **Leave:** Maximum 10 days per semester in exceptional cases (must be pre-sanctioned).
