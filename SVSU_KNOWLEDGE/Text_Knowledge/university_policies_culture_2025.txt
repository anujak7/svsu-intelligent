[SVSU UNIVERSITY POLICIES, CULTURE & ORDINANCES - 2025-26]

### The Legend of Lord Vishwakarma & University Inspiration
- **Inspiration:** Lord Vishwakarma is the architect of the universe and designer of divine chariots/weapons. SVSU takes inspiration from the **Chariot** (Rath), connected to the legend of Mahabharata in Haryana.
- **Architectural Symbolism:** 
  - The **six academic blocks** are conceived as the **horses** of the chariot (symbolizing purpose, speed, and dynamism).
  - The **administrative and auditorium blocks** represent the **balancing wheels** of the chariot.
- **City Planning:** Lord Vishwakarma created Dwaraka (Krishna's capital), Hastinapur (Kauravas), and Indraprastha (Pandavas).

### SVSU Core Values & Mission
- **Student-Centric:** Equal opportunities and high engagement with industry.
- **Knowledge Application:** Learning into practice (Dual Education Model).
- **Integrity:** Ethical and honest behavior without supervision.
- **Innovation & Inclusivity:** Disruptive thinking and respecting diversity.
- **Learning & Growth:** "Earn while you learn" model.
- **Industry Partners:** sustainable partnerships to change the skilling landscape.
- **New Age Technology:** Equipping students with future-oriented industrial know-how.

### Hostel Ordinance (Administrative & Financial)
- **Staffing:** Chief Hostel Warden (Regular Professor), Hostel Wardens (Regular Assoc/Asst Professor - 1 per 100 students), Supervisors, Caretakers, and Housekeeping.
- **Honorarium:** Chief Warden (Rs 5000/mo), Warden (Rs 3000/mo). Wardens get free accommodation on campus (no HRA deduction).
- **Hostel Finance Committee:** Members include Chief Warden, Wardens, and Chief Account Officer. Functions: Managing separate hostel accounts, urgent financial decisions, and purchasing.
- **Purchasing Power:** Chief Warden (up to Rs 1,50,000), Warden (up to Rs 20,000).
- **Committees:** 
  - **Admission:** Chief Warden + Wardens.
  - **Discipline:** Wardens + Residents.
  - **Mess:** Wardens + 2 Residents (Menu planning and hygiene).
  - **Cultural & Sports:** 7 Residents each.
  - **Cleanliness:** Wardens + Supervisor + 4 Residents.
  - **Anti-Ragging:** Chief Warden + Wardens + Supervisor + Seniors.
  - **Grievance:** Wardens + 4 Residents.

### Talent Attraction & Retention Policy (Staff/Faculty)
- **Flexible Work Environment:** 
  - **5-Day Working Week:** 36 weeks teaching, 8 weeks exams/admissions, 6 weeks vacations.
  - **Flexi-Hours:** Employees can avail flexible hours (max 4 times/month), but not between 10 AM - 3 PM.
  - **Work from Home (Online Mode):** Mothers/Fathers with babies up to 2 years can work online 1 day/week (max 30 days total) with prior approval.
- **Professional Development:** 
  - **Executive Education:** 5% of employees (Level A/B/C) with 2+ years service can pursue courses from premier institutes (15 days to 6 months).
  - **Extended Industry OJT:** 1-2 months training beyond mandatory 2 weeks.
